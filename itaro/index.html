Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Your privacy is taken seriously at Itaro. This policy outlines how we secure and handle your data.

1. Data Collection & Storage

We use Google Firebase to securely store and authenticate your data. We collect:

  • Account Info: Name and Email (for login and identifying you to friends).
  • Trip Data: Expenses, balances, and partial definitions manually entered by you.

2. Data Usage & Safety

Your data is used solely to facilitate expense splitting and sync across devices.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security infrastructure.

3. Data Deletion

You have full control. Permanently delete your account and all data instantly via the app: Profile > Account > Delete Account.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@itaro.app.
